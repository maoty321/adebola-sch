    About Us

    Adebola Excellent College of Health Sciences and Technology is a giant private College in Bode- Osi, Iwo, Ola Oluwa Local Government, Osun State, Nigeria. It's known as AECOHTECH. It's one of the best textiary institution in Nigeria. It is an Healthcare Professional institution that innovate educate and Heal. It was founded in 2024.
